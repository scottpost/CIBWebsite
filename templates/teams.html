    Software Development

    The Software Development Team is segmented in three main groups targeting different quantitative approach to finance.

    ⁃ The Portfolio Management Tools team implements tools designed for the portfolio management group and the club fund, backtest trading strategies and forecast metrics to facilitate investment decisions.
    ⁃ The Senior Quantitative Research team built a multi-stage model to identify companies with correlated implied volatility time series, then predict the magnitude of future implied volatility changes. They constructed a data pipeline that used the relationship model to generate labeled examples with future implied volatility changes of either (a) significant positive change (b) minimal change or (c) significant negative change. A neural network (using TensorFlow) was trained on these examples.
    ⁃ The Junior Software Engineering team is investigating relationships between public opinion and asset returns. To do so, they chose to query the Seeking Alpha website for articles of quality coming from analysts and non professionals. With a daily flux of data for most stocks, they hope to train classifiers to recognize patterns between returns and sentiment.They are first training Bayesian Classifiers on labelled IMDB data to detect positivity and subjectivity, using the TF-IDF feature generation method. Their goal is to find correlation between probabilities of class membership and market data. Eventually, they plan on training end-to-end recurrent networks using stock returns as labels and corresponding articles as inputs.
